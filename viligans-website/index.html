Specialized transportation to VA medical centers and VA-approved healthcare facilities. We understand the unique needs of veterans accessing their earned healthcare benefits.

Highly Rural Transportation Program

Platte County qualifies as a "highly rural" area under VA guidelines (fewer than 7 people per square mile), making our veterans eligible for specialized transportation grants and services.

No Cost to Veterans

Transportation services are provided at no charge to qualifying veterans through VA Highly Rural Transportation Grants and Wyoming Rural Transportation Special Needs funding.
